Ming Li

Assistant Professor

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Nevada, Reno (UNR). I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Mississippi State University in 2014. Before that, I received my M.E. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2010, and B.E. in Electrical Engineering from Sun Yat-Sen University in 2007, respectively.
